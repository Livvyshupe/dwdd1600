Know the Why

SOURCE

Explore the real values and motivation behind your desire to be successful with your fitness. DIG DEEP. Continue to refine your answer until you've come up with an answer that deeply resonates with you even if (and maybe especially if) it's something you're embarrassed or insecure about. Whenever you are being hard about yourself, REMEMBER THE WHY. One amazing quote to keep you going says,
“Do something today that your future self will thank you for.”

Important Factors When Regarding the why.

  1. Motivation
  2. Activity levels
  3. What drives you?
  4. How much time are you willing to commit?

What To Do Before Starting

Testing Form:
Form is essential in not getting hurt. Tests such as the Y test, McGill Torso Muscular Endurance Battery Test, and the Thomas Test can help improve form and determine what level you are at. This will prevent injury.
Muscular Strenth Tests:
Bend and lift, pulling motion, pushing motion, roatation, and single leg momements.
Cardiovascualr Endurance Tests:
The talk test determines how much you are exerting. Can you walk briskly for at least 20 minutes wthout stopping? If so you are ready to begin higher intensity cardio training.
